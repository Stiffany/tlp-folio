Fast Connect SEO

Fast Connect NZ

Summary

Design two Landing pages Power & Broadband for Fastconnect New Zealand to drive more traffic to the Fastconnect comparison portal. It turns out New Zealanders use ‘power companies’ far more than Australians. I was tasked to develop these two landing pages. I worked with a UX writer who provided me with all the title copy, page file names, body copy and meta data tags. ‘compare power companies’ instead of ‘compare electricity companies’. Making the pages accessible wasn’t a deliverable, but accessibility plays a large role in how google indexes your website. SEO went from 81 to 100. Accessibility didn’t improve, however the business has a greater understanding of the importance of accessibility and have prioritised a rebuild of the website.
