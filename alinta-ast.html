Proto personas

Alinta Energy

Summary

The Alinta Sales Tool will be used by: Alinta Employees: Sales Agent Admins, Sales Agents, Sales Operations, Product/Marketing (content management), 3rd Party Sales Channels: Sales Admin, Sales Agent.

I conducted interviews with Sales Agent Admins, Sales Agents, Sales Operations inbound & outbound, Kiosk sales teams and third-party sales agents. The Personas represent data I collected from the users.

I created Personas based on the characters from the tv series Mad Men.

User testing, Joan Holloway Persona (DOCX 16 KB)

Peggy Olson, Admin

Third party sales channel admin & Don's boss

  • Peggy's goal is to improve her teams performance
  • Peggy is the person who creates and manages user accounts and generates reports on behalf of her third party sales channel.
  • She is the main contact for technical support.

Don Draper, Sales Agent

Don's goal is to beat his sales targets

  • Don is the facilitator, he facilitates sales on behalf of a third party sales channel.
  • He is one of the many well performing agents supported by Peggy Olson.
  • As long as Don can close sales, how he does it is his own business.

Roger Sterling, Channel Admin

Alinta sales channel admin

  • Roger needs smoother onboarding. Roger is the senior resource of the new Alinta sales tool.
  • The channels he manages are unique and all have specific requirementsand structures: tele sales, door to door, lead generated, online sales.
  • Roger is the glue that keeps sales teams running.

Joan Holloway, Alinta Energy sales agent

Joan's idea of a great day at work is to meet her KPI's

  • Joan is an onshore and an offshore sales agent.
  • Joan signs up new customers and add on sells to existing Alinta Energy customers.
  • She's getting her head around everything but doesn't deal directly with any Alinta sales team staff.

Stakeholder Feedback on my Personas

After i have completed all my research i take my data & create personas & proto personas.

  • My research comes out of stakeholder whiteboard Sessions, one on one interviews with Alinta Sales Agents, groups of 3 to 5 sales agent user interviews, third party Sales Agents interviews and online survey feedback from our offshore agents.
  • My personas are used as a visual layout of the data i've gathered from my user testing sessions and survey submissions.
  • I use personas as a guide when i make design decisions they help me when i get stuck on an interaction or function.
  • They help me figure out my users needs, wants and desires.
